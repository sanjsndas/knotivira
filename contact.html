What Happens After You Contact Us?

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form or via email, our customer support team receives your message. We prioritize all communications and strive to provide a thoughtful and comprehensive response as quickly as possible. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Confirmation: You will typically receive an automated confirmation email acknowledging receipt of your message. This email will include a reference number for your inquiry, which you can use if you need to follow up.
  • Initial Review: Our team will review your message to understand the nature of your request. This helps us direct your inquiry to the most appropriate specialist within Knotivira, whether it's related to an order, a product detail, a pattern question, or a technical issue.
  • Personalized Response: We aim to provide a personalized response rather than generic automated replies. Our goal is to address your specific concerns with accuracy and care. Depending on the complexity of your question, this might involve a direct answer, a request for more information, or a redirection to a more suitable resource on our Site.
  • Response Time: We typically respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. During peak periods or for highly complex issues, our response time might be slightly longer, but we will always endeavor to keep you informed if there are significant delays. Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM IST, excluding public holidays. Inquiries received outside these hours will be addressed on the next business day.
  • Resolution: Our team will work diligently to resolve your issue or answer your question to your satisfaction. This may involve multiple exchanges of communication to ensure all aspects of your inquiry are thoroughly addressed. We are committed to finding solutions that meet your needs and enhance your Knotivira experience.
  • Feedback Loop: After your inquiry is resolved, we may occasionally send a brief survey to gather your feedback on our support service. Your input is invaluable in helping us continuously improve our customer care and ensure we are meeting your expectations.
